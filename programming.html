Programming

Programming is the process of creating software, applications, and systems by writing instructions in a programming language. It is one of the most important skills in the modern world, allowing individuals to solve complex problems and automate tasks.

Common programming languages include Python, Java, C++, and JavaScript, each of which has different strengths depending on the application. In this post, we'll explore various programming paradigms, the importance of clean code, and tips for becoming a better developer.
